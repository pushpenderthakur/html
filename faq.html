General

Kittrow is a secure platform for exchanging cryptocurrency and fiat currencies. You can easily buy crypto with your local money or sell crypto and receive fiat directly to your bank account. The process is simple: register, verify your identity, choose the currencies, send or receive funds — all in a few clicks.

Yes, identity verification (KYC) is required to ensure the security of all users and comply with regulatory standards. It only takes a few minutes and protects you from fraud.

Kittrow supports major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), and other popular assets. We regularly update the list to add new trusted coins.

You can receive popular fiat currencies such as EUR (Euro), USD (US Dollar), and others, depending on your region. We aim to support more local currencies in the future.

The minimum exchange amount depends on the selected currencies but usually starts from around 20 EUR or its equivalent. The maximum amount can vary depending on verification level and payment method.
