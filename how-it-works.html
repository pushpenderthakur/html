How does Kittrow exchange work

Step 1

Select crypto and fiat — quick and easy

Start by selecting the cryptocurrency you want to exchange and the fiat currency you want to receive. We support popular pairs and convenient payout options.

Step 2

Quick signup and identity verification

To ensure security and compliance, we need to know who we're working with. Signup takes just a few minutes, and verification is a one-time process.

Step 3

Send crypto to the provided wallet

Once verified, you’ll receive the wallet address. As soon as the funds arrive, we’ll begin processing your request. The entire process usually takes less than 30 minutes. In some cases, slight delays may occur due to network load.

Step 4

Receive your funds via your preferred method

Once we confirm receipt of the crypto, you'll receive your money via the selected method — bank card, account, or e-wallet. Fast and transparent. Your data is protected at every step using advanced encryption. We never share personal information with third parties.

Swap Crypto & Fiat Instantly

Buy crypto with your local currency or convert it back — safely and in seconds. Kittrow makes fiat-to-crypto exchange simple, secure, and lightning-fast. No hidden fees, no hassle.
